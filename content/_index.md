Hello everyone! Welcome to *The Personal Blog*, where I share interesting and fun incidents from my day-to-day life. As a Linux desktop user, I love talking about Linux and sharing my experiences with it. Since I'm a science student, you'll also find some blog posts about science-related topics here. Additionally, I’ll occasionally recommend books that I think are worth reading.

Along with my interest in technology, I’m also into video games and video editing. I like to share my thoughts on the games I’m playing, offer reviews, and talk about different aspects of game development. I also enjoy experimenting with video editing, sharing some tips, and discussing the open-source tools I use to create videos. I'm a fan of open-source software because it provides great tools and resources for everyone, and I enjoy exploring different projects that make work and creativity easier.
